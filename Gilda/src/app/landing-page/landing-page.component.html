Gilda

Gilda is an intuitive, understanding, and functional voice assistant designed for your car. Unlike current solutions, Gilda allows technology to empower and not hinder your driving experience. Our assistant gives the driver easy access to relevant, important driving information and commands, leveraging the APIs of commonly used apps, through a touchless and gazeless user interface. You control Gilda solely through the sound of your voice, allowing you to keep your smartphone in your cup holder and your eyes on the road.

The Gilda team loves to drive. The best drives are fun, memorable, hassle free, and personalized to you. We want to make the best drive every drive.
